Frequently Asked Questions

How can I change my booking?

Go to Manage Booking, enter your booking reference, and follow the steps to modify your flight.

Can I cancel my ticket and get a refund?

Refund eligibility depends on your fare type. Review your fare conditions in Manage Booking.

How do I add baggage after booking?

You can add checked baggage in Manage Booking before your flight.

When can I check in online?

Online check-in usually opens 24 to 48 hours before departure.
