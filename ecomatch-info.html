What is EcoMatch Score?

EcoMatch Score helps compare flights by combining value, convenience, and environmental impact in one clear view.

How EcoMatch Works

EcoMatch combines four factors to help surface flights that fit both the trip and the traveler.

EcoMatch brings together price, travel time, number of stops, and estimated CO2 emissions so it is easier to compare flights at a glance.

Score Breakdown

Each factor is considered as part of the overall match score.

Price

Lower fares can improve the score when they still offer a strong overall trip option.

Travel Time

Shorter total journey times may score higher when they reduce overall travel effort.

Stops

Nonstop or lower-stop itineraries may rank better for convenience and reliability.

CO2 Emissions

Lower estimated emissions can improve the match for travelers comparing environmental impact.

How CO2 Is Estimated

The environmental view is intended to help comparison, not replace official airline reporting.

CO2 values are estimated using route distance and average emissions benchmarks for similar flights. They are provided as a comparison aid so travelers can see which options are relatively lower or higher impact.
